# DESIGN.md — Promptwars MVP

## Visual Theme & Atmosphere
"Modern, sleek, and technical. Dark mode by default with high-contrast accents. Uses glassmorphism and subtle gradients to feel like a premium AI tool."

## Color Palette & Roles
- **Primary BG:** #0f172a (Deep Slate)
- **Card BG:** rgba(30, 41, 59, 0.7) (Translucent Slate)
- **Accent Primary:** #38bdf8 (Sky Blue) — CTAs and Highlights
- **Accent Secondary:** #818cf8 (Indigo) — Secondary buttons
- **Text Main:** #f8fafc (Off-white)
- **Text Dim:** #94a3b8 (Slate Gray)

## Typography Rules
- **Font:** 'Inter', system-ui, sans-serif
- **Headings:** Bold, letter-spacing: -0.02em
- **Body:** 16px, line-height: 1.6

## Component Stylings
- **Buttons:** Rounded-xl (12px), transition: 200ms ease-in-out
- **Cards:** 24px radius, 1px border: rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.1), backdrop-filter: blur(12px)
